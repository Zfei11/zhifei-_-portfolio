ABOUT ME

ZHIFEI LUO

Hi, I’m Fei—a multidisciplinary designer from China with a deep passion for tattoos, painting, and all things creative.

As an interactive designer, I specialize in UX/UI Design and Digital Design, crafting engaging digital experiences that blend logic with artistry.

Education & Background

Degrees in one hand, a paintbrush in the other. I hold a Bachelor of Design Computing and a Master of Interactive Design & Digital Art, both from the University of Sydney—credentials that taught me how to marry code with poetry. But my real education? It’s in the grit of tattoo ink and the rebellion of a paint-splattered canvas.

Habbits & Motivation

I don’t just “blend aesthetics and functionality.” I wireframe like a tattoo artist sketches skin—every line deliberate, every interaction permanent in its purpose. My designs aren’t sterile; they’re stained with the raw energy of painting, where “mistakes” become textures and human flaws turn into hooks.

My AMBITIONS

I focus on precision execution, ensuring every project is fully functional, optimized, and ready to make an impact. The result? A professional and captivating portfolio that elevates your presence, showcases your talents, and leaves a lasting impression.
